## 💡 Tips for Success

### Project Building

1. **Start small** — Get something working first
2. **Use version control** — Commit often with meaningful messages
3. **Write README** — Document your projects well
4. **Handle errors** — Make your code robust
5. **Write tests** — Prove your code works

### Interview Preparation

1. **Practice daily** — Solve 1-2 problems per day
2. **Think aloud** — Explain your thought process
3. **Ask clarifying questions** — Show analytical thinking
4. **Know complexity** — Understand Big O
5. **Review fundamentals** — Data structures & algorithms
